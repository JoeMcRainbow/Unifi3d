About Unifi3d

Unifi3d is a project that was started by Anden Wieseler in 2020 as an extension of a writeup that he wrote for the iOS jailbreaking community (see here).

Building on the suggestions from r/jailbreak users, he decided to stop editing the writeup, and start incorporating the requests into a website format.

What is Unifi3d?

Unifi3d is a home for the facts in the iOS Jailbreaking community. Content that you might find on this site may include:

  • Major tweak/theme releases

  • Top tweaks as decided by the community (more info on this once Unifi3d is launched)

  • Release info for new jailbreak tools

  • Tutorials and writeups written by community members

  • Important community information

  • Important developer/designer tweets

The best part?

The content is comunity driven.

Community driven? What do you mean?

Upon the launch of Unifi3d, there will be a feedback page, where members of the community can fill out a Google Form to reflect on what they like about the site, what they don't like, bug reports, and even content requests! This site is for the community, so why shouldn't the community get a say?

How will this mitigate the drama from the r/jailbreak community?

Great question. As mentioned before, there will be a Google Form for feedback, as a replacement for an open comments section. This decision was made to prevent the spread of negativity, and to support the idea that all ideas are welcomed and appreciated.

More info on this matter will be given once Unifi3d is launched completely.
